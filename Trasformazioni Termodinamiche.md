#FIS 
### Isobara
- $W = p \Delta V$
- $Q = nC_{p}\Delta T$
- $\Delta U = nC_{v}\Delta T$
- $\Delta S = nC_{p}\ln\left( \frac{T_{2}}{T_{1}} \right)$
- $\frac{V_{1}}{T_{1}}=\frac{V_{2}}{T_{2}}$

### Isocora
- $W = 0$
- $Q = \Delta U = nC_{v}\Delta T$
- $\Delta S = nC_{v}\ln\left( \frac{T_{2}}{T_{1}} \right)$
- $\frac{p_{1}}{T_{1}} = \frac{p_{2}}{T_{2}}$

### Isoterma
- $W = Q = nRT\ln\left( \frac{V_{2}}{V_{1}} \right)$
- $\Delta U = 0$
- $\Delta S = -nR\ln\left( \frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right) = nR\ln\left( \frac{V_{2}}{V_{1}} \right)$
### Adiabatica
- $Q = 0$
- $W = -\Delta U = -nC_{v}\Delta T = \frac{p_{1}V_{1} - p_{2}V_{2}}{\gamma-1}$
- $\Delta S = nC_{v}\ln\left( \frac{T_{2}}{T_{1}} \right) + nR\ln\left( \frac{V_{2}}{V_{1}} \right) = nC_{p}\ln\left( \frac{T_{2}}{T_{1}} \right) - nR\ln\left( \frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right)$
#### Isoentropica (Reversibile)
- $pV^\gamma =\text{Costante}$
- $TV^{\gamma-1} =\text{Costante}$
- $T^\gamma p^{1 - \gamma} =\text{Costante}$
- $\Delta S = 0$
