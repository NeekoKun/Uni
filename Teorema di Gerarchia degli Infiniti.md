#analisi 
> [!error] Teorema
> $\forall\ \alpha>0,a>1:$
> 1) $\lim_{ n \to \infty }\dfrac{\log_{a}n}{n^\alpha} = 0$
> 2) $\lim_{ n \to \infty }\dfrac{n^\alpha}{a^n} = 0$
> 3) $\lim_{ n \to \infty }\dfrac{a^n}{n!} = 0$

> [!info] Osservazione
> $$\lim_{ n \to \infty } \dfrac{\sin n^2}{n^\alpha} = 0$$
> $b_{n} = \frac{1}{n^\alpha}, c_{n}=\sin n^2$
> $\implies \lim_{ n \to \infty }b_{n} = 0, |c_{n}|\leq 1$
> $\implies b_{n}c_{n}\to 0$

- Ne deriva la [[Gerarchia degli Infiniti]]
